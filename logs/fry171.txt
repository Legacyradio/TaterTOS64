Change: Force PMM bitmap placement below 4GB during early boot.
Why: Early page table construction relies on identity mapping of low memory; if the bitmap is placed above 4GB, writes go to unmapped memory and corrupt data/return addresses, leading to non-canonical RIP faults.
Details:
- pmm.c: limit bitmap candidate ranges to <4GB and clip ranges that cross 4GB.
