Evidence
- QEMU log shows RIP = 0007fdffffffff80 and RSP = ffffffff80016e90 during #GP/#DF loop.
- IDT base is ffffffff80016e20 (nm: idt at 0xffffffff80016e20).
- Kernel stack is 64 KiB: kernel_stack [0xffffffff80019ea0 .. 0xffffffff80029ea0].
- RSP is below kernel_stack base by ~0x3010, indicating stack overflow into IDT region.

Changes
1) Increased kernel stack size from 64 KiB to 256 KiB.
   - src/kernel/main.c: kernel_stack[262144]

   - Why: prevent stack overflow from ACPI/AML and early init phases corrupting IDT.
