Handoff Follow-up - 2026-02-21

Summary
- Moved kernel stack into its own section, aligned it to a 2MB boundary, and mapped it separately with a guard page.
- Goal: prevent stack overflow from corrupting IDT and force a clean #PF instead.

Changes
1) src/kernel/main.c
   - Placed kernel_stack in dedicated .stack section.

2) kernel_linker.ld
   - Added .stack section aligned to 2MB.
   - Added __kernel_stack_base/__kernel_stack_top and LMA symbols.
   - Kernel end remains before stack; LMA end now includes stack.

3) src/kernel/mm/vmm.c
   - Map kernel higher-half without stack using 2MB pages.
   - Map stack separately with 4KB pages and leave a guard page at the base.

Why
- QEMU logs show non-canonical RIP and RSP dipping into IDT memory.
- Separating the stack and adding a guard page prevents silent IDT corruption.
