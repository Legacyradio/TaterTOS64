Handoff Follow-up - 2026-02-21

Summary
- Aligned LMA of .stack and .iststack to 4KB to avoid mis-mapped physical pages.

Evidence
- nm showed __ist_stacks_lma_start not page-aligned (0x2c5dc5), which breaks page mapping
  because vmm_map_range drops low 12 bits of phys address.

Changes
1) kernel_linker.ld
   - .stack LMA aligned to 0x1000.
   - .iststack LMA aligned to 0x1000.
   - __kernel_lma_end aligned to 0x1000.

Why
- IST and stack pages must be page-aligned for correct mapping. Misalignment prevents IST
  from working and can cause #PF/#DF loops.
