Summary
- Moved IST stacks to low identity-mapped .boot section so #PF/#DF can always switch to a safe stack.

Changes
1) src/boot/tss.c
   - Place ist1_stack/ist2_stack in .boot section (low memory identity map).

2) src/kernel/mm/vmm.c
   - Removed .iststack mapping (IST stacks no longer in high-half section).

Why
- IST stacks were in high-half and still not being used; putting them in low identity map
  avoids any high-half mapping issues during early faults.
