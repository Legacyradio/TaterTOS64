Changes (already present in tree)
1) src/drivers/storage/nvme.c
   - Ensure MMIO BAR is mapped: vmm_ensure_physmap(bar + 0x2000)
   - Access BAR via vmm_phys_to_virt(bar)
   - Convert pmm_alloc_page() results to virtual pointers with vmm_phys_to_virt() for:
     - identify buffer
     - admin SQ/CQ
     - IO SQ/CQ

Why
- In QEMU the NVMe BAR can be at 0xC000000000 (>> 4GB); without physmap extension
  the MMIO registers are unmapped.
- pmm_alloc_page returns physical addresses; need vmm_phys_to_virt for CPU access.
