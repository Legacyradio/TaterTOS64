[2026-02-22 08:51:00 UTC] Step: Support NVMe PRP2 for cross-page buffers.
Why: GPT/FAT32 reads use stack buffers that may span two pages; without PRP2 the driver rejects the read, causing GPT header read failure.
Change: If a read/write buffer crosses a page boundary, compute PRP2 from the next page's virtual address and include it in the command.
