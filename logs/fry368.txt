     File: src/kernel/acpi/power.c

       - attempts ACPI S5 writes,
       - attempts QEMU/Bochs poweroff fallback (outw 0x0604, 0x2000),
       - never returns to normal flow; halts forever if poweroff does not occur.
     - Removed prior "sleep then kernel_panic" shutdown-failure behavior.
