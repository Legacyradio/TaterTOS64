fry371 — Stability hardening: disable AP LAPIC periodic timers in BSP-only scheduler mode
Timestamp: 2026-02-28

Why:
  User reports long-running instability after the shell-input fix campaign.
  Current scheduler intentionally runs runnable user processes on BSP (CPU0)
  only, but APs were still enabling LAPIC periodic timers in ap_entry().
  That creates continuous AP interrupt traffic without any scheduling benefit
  and increases cross-core race surface.

Change:
  File: src/drivers/smp/smp.c
  - Removed AP-side lapic_timer_init() call from ap_entry().
  - Added comment documenting that AP timers stay disabled until per-CPU
    scheduling is implemented.

Expected effect:
  - Lower interrupt contention/churn on APs.
  - Better stability on bare metal during GUI app launch/input paths.
