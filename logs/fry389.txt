fry389 — ACPI diagnostics syscall + sysinfo display
Timestamp: 2026-02-28 ~17:15 UTC

WHAT:
Added SYS_ACPI_DIAG (syscall 28) to expose ACPI discovery status to userspace.
Sysinfo app now shows a "--- ACPI Diagnostics ---" panel with:
  - EC: OK/FAIL + port addresses (0x62/0x66 or alternate)
  - Battery: count of found nodes, _STA flags, namespace path
  - Backlight: found flag, _STA flags, namespace path, BCL level count,
    supported count, raw value, percent

WHY:
On bare metal Dell Precision 7530 there is no serial output, so all kprint()
diagnostics during ACPI init are invisible. Battery shows "unavailable" and
backlight shows 0% despite being cranked in BIOS. Need on-screen visibility
into whether ACPI even found the battery/backlight nodes, and EC status.
