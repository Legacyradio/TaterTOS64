     - rejects non-plausible EC ports/pairs (including low placeholder ports),
     - strongly prefers 0x62/0x66, then legacy alternate 0x68/0x6C,
     - otherwise selects best plausible pair while keeping non-standard port support.
   - Added verbose _CRS candidate logging and explicit rejection log when no
     plausible EC pair is found.
   - Preserves existing fallback chain:
     selected-node _CRS -> ECDT -> default 0x62/0x66.

WHY:
  This prevents bad _CRS templates from forcing EC to 0x00/0x04 while keeping
  compatibility across different machines by still allowing plausible
  non-standard EC ports.
