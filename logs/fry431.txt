=======================================================================
Date: 2026-03-01 18:31:14 UTC

WHAT CHANGED (why)
=======================================================================
- `src/kernel/acpi/ec.c`
  - Added `ec_probe_step_name()` helper for compact failure summaries.
  - Updated `ec_probe_verbose()` to keep diagnostic state updates but stop
    printing one line per retry/failure step.
  - Updated `ec_probe_with_retries()` to print a single summary line:
    - success: one `EC probe ok ...` line with tries/status
    - failure: one `EC probe fail ...` line with tries/final-step/status
  - Why: prevent ACPI/EC retry spam from flooding the on-screen log window
    while preserving actionable diagnostics.
