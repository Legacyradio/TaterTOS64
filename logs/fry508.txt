TaterTOS64v3 - fry508 — NTFS MFT cache + mount hygiene
========================================================================

TIMESTAMP
---------
2026-03-03 18:09:37 CST

WHY THIS LOG
------------
- Improve NTFS directory perf and safety by caching MFT records and handling $DATA-less directories.
- Prevent NVMe remount state leakage by freeing secondary fs_data on unmount and capping mount count.

CHANGES
-------
- Added a 64-entry LRU cache of fixup-applied MFT records to avoid re-reading $MFT on large readdir()s.
- Guarded ntfs_get_file_info(): directories without $DATA now report size=0 without falling back to FILE_NAME sizes.
- Implemented vfs_umount() that frees secondary fs_data (including NTFS cache buffers) and exposes the API.
- Added mount prefix counting with a hard cap (8) to stop unbounded /nvmeX mount growth during recovery attempts.
