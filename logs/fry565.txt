WHY
---
- Namespace node names are fixed-size ACPI NameSeg values:
  - `struct acpi_node { char name[4]; ... }` in `src/kernel/acpi/namespace.h`
- Node names are copied as exactly 4 bytes on creation:
  - `set_name(dst[4], src[4])` and `set_name(n->name, name)` in
    `src/kernel/acpi/namespace.c`
- AML parser also reads NameSeg as exactly 4 bytes:
  - `parse_name_seg(..., char name[4])` in `src/kernel/acpi/aml_parse.c`
- ACPI short names are represented via underscore padding, so reading indices
  `[0..3]` is correct by model and by implementation.
