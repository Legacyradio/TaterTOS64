-------
- Review flagged race potential in `create_acpi_device()` due to shared static
  arrays (`devs`, `names`) and `dev_count` with no synchronization.

WHY
---
- Without a lock, concurrent callers can observe/stomp the same `dev_count`
  slot and interleave writes to shared arrays.
- Even if current call sites are typically single-threaded, hardening this path
  avoids future regressions if enumeration context changes.

    - Removed trailing unlocked `dev_count++`.

- Ensures each caller gets a unique slot and consistent name/device pair.
