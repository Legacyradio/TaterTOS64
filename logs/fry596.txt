ROOT CAUSE OF MISSING MARKERS
------------------------------
- fry595 tried physmap (0xFFFF800000000000 + fb_base) — crashed because FB MMIO
  is not in the physmap range.
- But VMM_FB_BASE (0xFFFFFFFFB0000000) was ALREADY mapped during vmm_init()
  (vmm.c line 419-422) with vmm_map_range(). This address lives in PML4[511],
  which is in the higher-half range (entries 256-511) that vmm_create_address_space()
  copies to every user page table.
- Therefore VMM_FB_BASE is accessible from syscall context under user CR3.

FIX
---
- Changed boot_diag_stage() in syscall.c to use VMM_FB_BASE (0xFFFFFFFFB0000000)
  instead of physmap or identity mapping.
- Removed the identity-limit guard check (not needed for VMM_FB_BASE).
- Re-enabled boot_diag_stage() calls in:
  - note_user_boot_progress(): stage 36 (K_FIRST_USER_SYSCALL), stage 37 (K_INIT_SYSCALL)
  - SYS_SPAWN: stage 38 (K_INIT_GUI_SPAWN)
  - SYS_FB_INFO / SYS_FB_MAP: stage 39 (K_GUI_FB)
