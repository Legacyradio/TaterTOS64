FIX: COLORED SPAWN DIAGNOSTIC SQUARES
--------------------------------------
Added a second row of colored 12x12 squares at y=20 (below the white boot
marker row at y=0). Each SYS_SPAWN attempt draws one square at column N:

  YELLOW = SYS_SPAWN entered (before copy_user_string)
  RED    = spawn failed (copy_user_string failed OR process_launch returned <0)
  GREEN  = spawn succeeded (process_launch returned valid PID)

Also added boot_diag_color(col, row, color) helper alongside boot_diag_stage().
Uses same VMM_FB_BASE approach (safe under user CR3).

Serial output also added: "SPAWN_ENTER path=...", "SPAWN_OK", "SPAWN_FAIL"
(visible in QEMU serial log for debugging).
