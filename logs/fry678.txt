  the 9260 `_8000` direct FH upload flow, and the register definitions were
  wrong:

    - Linux `iwl-prph.h` defines `0x1E78` / `0x1E7C`

  The trace values (`0x5`, `0xcafcafbd`) are not valid header locations and
  indicated the handoff instrumentation was looking at the wrong place while
  also writing into the wrong place. For the 9260 direct FH upload path, keep
  these as read-only diagnostics instead of programming them here.
