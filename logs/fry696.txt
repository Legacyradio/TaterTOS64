    - `init-alive-fail`
  then the lead shifts back to the INIT boot / ALIVE exit boundary.

  The real hardware run sharpened that branch further:
    - both retained entries were `init-alive-fail`
    - `hostcmd=0`

      `alive-rekick` / `alive-timeout`
