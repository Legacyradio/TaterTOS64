fry730 — Scheduler Logging Backfill
===================================
Timestamp: 2026-03-14 20:55:07 CDT

Backfill for the earlier unlogged scheduler investigation patch.

What changed:
- `src/kernel/proc/sched.c`
- Added a BSP bootstrap pseudo-task (`boot_tasks`) and seeded `current[bsp]`
  from it instead of the BSP idle task.
- Added `process_reap_deferred_stacks()` after `context_switch()`.

Why:
- The intent was to stop the BSP from saving the first live boot-thread stack
  into the idle task state and to make deferred kernel-stack reclamation happen
  after task switches.

Result:
- QEMU reproduced the same `RIP=0x100B` invalid-opcode failure, so that change
  was not sufficient.
