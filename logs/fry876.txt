  - writev/pwritev
  - dl_iterate_phdr
  - pthread_* helpers
  - getenv
  - __xpg_strerror_r
- Adding catch-all fake syscall/libc stubs would violate the no-shortcuts rule, so I did not wire the host archive into the active Makefile as a final port step.

Current Decision:
