Key Fixes
1) g_handoff copied to heap after heap_init
- File: src/kernel/main.c
- Why: Avoid low-memory handoff page access when running under user CR3.

2) PMM bitmap relocated to physmap after VMM init
- Files: src/kernel/mm/pmm.c, src/kernel/mm/pmm.h, src/kernel/main.c
- Why: Bitmap originally lives in low memory; user CR3 doesn't map it, causing #PF -> reboot.

3) NVMe sync on build + non-interactive overwrite
- File: build_iso.sh
- Changes:
  - Sync GUI/apps into out/tater_nvme.img (FAT at LBA 2048)

4) PS/2 keyboard fallback polling
- File: src/drivers/input/ps2_kbd.c
- Change: If IRQ buffer empty, poll 0x64/0x60 briefly to capture keypress.
