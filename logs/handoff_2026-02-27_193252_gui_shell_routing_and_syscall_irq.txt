Implemented:
1) GUI-mode stdout routing fix
   - File: src/kernel/proc/syscall.c
   - SYS_WRITE(fd=1) no longer mirrors non-TaterWin user stdout to framebuffer
     while GUI process is running.

   - Console fallback remains active when GUI is not running.

2) Hot-path efficiency improvement
   - File: src/kernel/proc/syscall.c
   - Added cached GUI process hint (slot/pid) to avoid full process-table scans
     on every stdout write in normal steady state.

3) Syscall execution hardening
   - File: src/kernel/proc/syscall.c (syscall_entry asm)
   - Enabled IRQs during syscall_dispatch (`sti`) and masked IRQs only during
     return-state restore (`cli` + sysret path).
   - Keeps stack-switch safety while reducing long-syscall stall/deadlock risk.
