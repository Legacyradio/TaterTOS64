Precision 7530 (which has no serial output). Also improved EC init

CHANGES MADE
-------------
1. ec.c: Probe diagnostic tracking — captures which step failed
   (STS_FF/IBF_PRE/IBF_POST/OBF_TIMEOUT/OK), raw status byte, attempt count.
   New: ec_get_probe_diag(), ec_reset_fail_count().

2. extended.c: EC init tracking — records whether PNP0C09 was found,
   _REG was called, _INI was found, _GPE was found/number. Added ~100ms
   delay after _REG before probe. Resets consecutive failure counter
   before ec_probe() (prevents AML-triggered failures from disabling EC).

3. extended.h + libc.h: fry_acpi_diag struct extended with 8 new EC
   diagnostic fields (appended at end, no layout breakage).

4. sysinfo.c: Two new diagnostic lines showing:
   - "probe: OBF_TMO sts=0x20 try=5" — which step failed
   - "node=1 REG=1 INI=0 GPE=1/6" — which init steps ran
   Window height increased from 340 to 380.
